FAQ

Things people ask before picking a tier

Why are these prices below market?
Obsidian Studio is a new studio building its portfolio. Founding-client rates are intentionally below Ontario-market norms and rise as our case-study list grows. Locking in at today's number locks you out of that rise. Early clients get genuine value and a rate we won't be able to offer again.
Why are these prices ranges, not a single number?
Final cost depends on content readiness (do you have photos, copy, reviews ready or do we source them?), the number of service-area pages you want generated, and how many revision rounds you want baked in. The fixed-price proposal is sent after you pick a tier — no surprise invoices.
Do I own the website after it's built?
Yes. Domain, code, images, copy — yours. We retain none of it. If you walk away, everything comes with you.
How long does a build take?
Starter: 7–10 days. Growth: 14–21 days. Pro: 21–35 days. These assume content handoff (photos, logo, copy, reviews) happens within the first 3 days of kickoff.
What if I don't know which tier fits?
Reply "just curious" to the email you got this from (or send a fresh one via the form below). I'll send a 3-question diagnostic so we can pick together in five minutes.
What does the Care Plan actually cover?
Hosting + security monitoring + uptime alerts; up to 2 hours per month of content updates; quarterly SEO check-ins and Lighthouse audits; a monthly form + analytics summary; priority support. Billed monthly, cancel any time.
